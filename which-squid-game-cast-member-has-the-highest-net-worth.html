Which Squid Game Cast Member Has The Highest Net Worth?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, August 11, 2024

When it comes to Netflix series, it's no surprise that the newest addition, Squid Game is on its way to becoming one of the most-watched shows of all time. The Korean series, written by Hwang Dong-hyuk, was first pitched over a decade ago, however, it was rejected by countless studios until it was finally picked up by Netflix.

Fans have been falling in love with the show, however, it's clear they've been falling in love with the hilarious cast more! With a few familiar faces from hit Korean series, to a number of new actors on the scene, there's no denying the impact Squid Game has had on the cast, both to their careers and bank accounts.

Related: Who Is Lee Jung-jae? What We Know About The 'Squid Game' Actor

With the series ranking in the top 10 across the globe, fans are wondering just how rich the show has made the cast, and better yet, who comes out on top with the highest net worth?

Lee Byung-hun Is Worth $20 Million

While we may not have seen his face too many times throughout the series, the Front Man, also known as Hwang In-ho was played by none other than South Korean icon, Lee Byung-hun. Fans got to know him mostly from his black mask and voice throughout, however, his identity was revealed only moments before Hwang shot his own brother.

Lee Byung-hun is no stranger to the big screen, considering he's appeared in many film and television shows before, including Joint Security Area (2000), A Bittersweet Life (2005), Masquerade (2012), The Magnificent Seven (2016), and of course, Squid Game. The actor is recognized throughout South Korea, making it no surprise that he's managed to amass a net worth of $20 million, making him the richest Squid Game cast member.

Lee Jung-jae Is Worth $5 Million

Lee Jung-jae played none other than the lead in Squid Game, Seong Gi-Hun, also known as Player 456. While he may have been eager to earn billions of won in the sadistic child games, it's evident that Lee Jung-jae is hardly desperate for the money. The actor is quite known throughout South Korea, having debuted back in 1994 in the series, Feelings.

Lee was later cast in the K-drama Sandglass, before earning himself his breakout role in An Affair, which instantly led his fan base to skyrocket. Today, the actor is now known worldwide for his portrayal of Seong Gi-Hun, a chauffeur with a gambling addiction. Luckily for Jung-jae, he couldn't be farther removed from his character, considering the actor is worth $5 million.

'Squid Game' Turned Heo Sung-tae Into A Millionaire

While Heo Sung-tae may have played the on-screen villain, Jang Deok-su, he is quite the sweetheart in real life! The star shared an image of himself behind the scenes on set holding a bouquet of players, thanking his fans in the caption for "making him a millionaire."

The hilarious shot had fans falling in love with the actor more and more, and with the success of Squid Game, it's no surprise that he's managed to earn himself quite the paycheck, putting his net worth anywhere between $1 - $2 million.
