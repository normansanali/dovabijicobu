Kacey Musgraves family, husband, children, parents, siblings

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 24, 2024

Kacey Lee Musgraves is an American country singer. In this article, we will learn about her as we dive into her personal life to know her family – husband, children, parents, and siblings. Scroll to the bottom to know almost everything about the singer’s family.

Table of Content

Kacey Musgraves husband

Kacey as of June 2022, is a divorcee. She was married to Ruston Samuel Kelly – an American singer-songwriter. They were married for just three years as they tied the knot in 2027 and separated in 2020. According to them, they parted ways because it didn’t just work out for them.

Kacey Musgraves children

Kacey has no child. She is believed to be focusing on her music career for now and will probably make time for giving birth when maybe she remarries.

Kacey Musgraves parents

Karen Musgraves and Craig Musgraves are the beautiful couples who gave birth to the county singer. Craig is the owner of a small printing business, M-Prints Printing, Inc., in Mineola, Texas, and Karen Musgraves is an artist.

Kacey Musgraves siblings

Kacey is from a family of two siblings who are all girls, hence she has a sister. Kacey happens to be the first child and daughter of her parents, hence, her sister identified as Kelly Christine Sutton is the youngest and happens to be a photographer.
