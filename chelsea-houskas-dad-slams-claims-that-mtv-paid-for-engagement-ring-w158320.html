Chelsea Houskas Dad Slams Claims That MTV Paid for Engagement Ring

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, September 18, 2024

Instead of “He went to Jared!” did Cole DeBoer go to MTV while preparing to propose to Chelsea Houska? Not according to the Teen Mom 2 star’s wise and loving father, Randy Houska.

On Sunday, Nov. 22, he tweeted, “Finally got to see @ChelseaHouska in person yesterday. Cole did pretty good on #THEROCK. Welcome to the crazy #houskaworld Cole.”

One jaded commenter replied, “You mean MTV did good?!?” which prompted Randy to respond, “No, I mean Cole did good #readwhatIwrote.”

The live-in loves announced their engagement via Instagram on Nov. 18, when Chelsea flashed her giant diamond sparkler for the camera.

“He's stuck now! @coledeboer,” she captioned a selfie with her man. “I honestly cannot believe how lucky I am. Finally found my Prince Charming and I get to be with him for the rest of my life!”

At the time, Randy praised his future son-in-law, tweeting, “Prince Charming > toad Ready to add another #soninlaw Lesson to you all: #DoNotSettleForLessThanYouDeserve Congrats @ChelseaHouska & Cole.”

It also looks like the MTV cameras were either rolling or nearby when the proposal happened, so Chelsea fans will get a glimpse of the romantic moment — and a reaction from Aubree, her 6-year-old daughter with ex Adam Lind.
