Martha Stewart Net Worth, Movies And TV Shows, Age

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, July 4, 2024

Martha Helen Stewart is an American retail businesswoman, writer, and television personality.

As founder of Martha Stewart Living Omnimedia, she gained success through a variety of business ventures, encompassing publishing, broadcasting, merchandising, and e-commerce.

Martha Stewart Net Worth

Martha Stewart’s net worth is estimated to be in the vicinity of $400 million as of 2020. Martha Stewart first became a billionaire when her company, Martha Stewart Living Omnimedia, went public in 1999.
