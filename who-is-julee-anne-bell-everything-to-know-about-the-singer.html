Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, July 31, 2024

She was born blind but hasn’t let her disability slow her career progress till now. In addition, she is a singing teacher, classically trained singer, and the managing director of World Access For The Blind.

The Voice Julee-Anne Bell Age

Julee-Anne Bell is 48 years old inspiration who was born in 1973.

Furthermore, she is Australian.

Julee-Anne Bell Wikipedia

The official Wikipedia site hasn’t managed to upload Julee-Anne Bell’s full bio profile.

She sang the song Climb Ev’ry Mountain from The Sound of Music in the blind audition of The Voice Australia Season 10. Her performance brought tears into the judges and her husband’s eyes.

Later the judge, Guy Sebastian stood on the stage with her and sang the song together. Also according to an interview, Julee’s favorite judge is Guy.

Julee was born with the disability of blindness and hasn’t been able to perceive light her entire life. Nonetheless, she is one of the stars and a teacher who has been a light to others.

She is a degreed choir director and a vocal instructor who coaches students from her home in Australia. Similarly, she also works as a Managing director of World Access For The Blind.

There is also a YouTube channel with her name which often uploads her recorded songs and performances.

Julee-Anne Bell Husband & Family

The vocalist Julee-Anne Bell is married to her husband Thomas, who is the most important person in her life.

Thomas is also the same age as her, and that is 48. Furthermore, her husband was the one who helped her go to the audition of The Voice. 

While Julee was helping one of her friends fill-up the form for an audition, her husband encouraged her to apply as well.

Her family has gone through a lot over the years. In 2011, her family’s Queensland home was hit by a devastating flood. The flood ended destroying all family photos and the base level of the home.

Julee at present raises her two children with her husband, Thomas.

Julee-Anne Bell Instagram

Her Instagram account is available as @juleeannebellmusic.

Furthermore, it is the official account of her vocal studio named Julee-Anne Bell Music. It has over 600 followers at the moment.
