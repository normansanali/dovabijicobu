Jessica Green

$500 thousand

Jessica Green is an Australian actress. Her career in entertainment began in 2012, first playing the role of Kiki in the television series Lightning Point. She next made her first movie role in 2014’s Rise, after which she appeared in the film Red Billabong. In 2017, she had a role in the television film Dirty, Clean, & Inbetween, as well as appeared as an extra in Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Men Tell No Tales. In 2018, she portrayed Cleopatra in the documentary Roman Empire. More recently, Green gained mainstream recognition for her lead role as Talon in the television series The Outpost.
