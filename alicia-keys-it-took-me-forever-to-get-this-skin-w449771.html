Alicia Keys: It Took Me Forever to Get This Skin

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Loving the skin she’s in. Alicia Keys stuck to her no-cosmetics vow by gracing Elle UK’s December 2016 issue without a trace of makeup on her face.

The “Hallelujah” singer, 35, wore her curly hair loose in the black-and-white shot that commemorates the magazine’s Feminism issue. Inside, she also models a teal head wrap, a black-and-white-striped blouse and silver “A” initial earrings. In another photo, the Voice coach shows even more skin in a black off-the-shoulder top with a red and black printed scarf to accessorize her mane.

'It took me forever to get this skin,” she said in the issue, according to the Daily Mail. “I mean, I know how it feels to have a face full of bumps and pimples. I lived through that in the public eye.”

Keys, who was a Proactiv spokesmodel, maintained that going without makeup isn’t too much of a sacrifice. “Some have called this a crusade,” she continued, affirming the fact that she is a feminist. “Well, I died laughing at that. I used to feel the pressure to appear as the music industry expected, but I don't now.”

In fact, the singer, who shares two children with music producer Swizz Beatz, has learned a few tricks of the trade to keep her complexion clear. “Alicia gets regular facials, does acupuncture and she eats healthy and exercises,” her makeup artist Dotti told W Magazine. She also uses ice to “bring her skin to life,” cucumbers to soothe her eyes, and pure jojoba oil to moisturize.
