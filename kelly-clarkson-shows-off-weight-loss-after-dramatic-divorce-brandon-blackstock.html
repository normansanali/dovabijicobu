Kelly Clarkson Debuts Slender Figure As She Shes Working On Herself After Heartbreaking Div

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, July 27, 2024

Highlights

  • Kelly Clarkson's recent photos showcase her noticeably slender figure, with fans showering her with compliments on her appearance and apparent weight loss.
  • There is speculation that Kelly's Las Vegas residency and high-energy shows may have played a part in her body transformation.
  • In the past, Kelly revealed that she lost 40 pounds by following a specific diet plan for health reasons, which resolved her thyroid issue and allowed her to stop taking medication.

Kelly Clarkson recently revealed that she’s been focusing on herself post-divorce, and the photos prove it. The singer was recently seen filming near the the plaza at Rockefeller Center in New York, showcasing a noticeably slender figure.

The musician was dressed in head-to-black, including skinny jeans, a structured blazer, and stiletto heels. Her long, blonde hair was styled straight in a simple ‘do. Paparazzi captured Kelly as she focused on talking at her filming crew, though it was unclear if she’s filming a segment for her talk show The Kelly Clarkson Show or doing something for another project.

Kelly’s Fans Believe She’s Looking Better Than Ever Post-Divorce

Close

This isn’t the first time recently Kelly has made waves for sporting a slender frame. Kelly took to Instagram on Saturday, October 7, to post a clip promoting an upcoming charity event. In the comments, Kelly’s followers were quick to shower her with compliments, admiring her appearance and noting her apparent weight loss.

“The AUDACITY to look that good in corduroy overalls,” one person commented, OK! Magazine notes. Another follower wrote, “Ok Kelly getting her s--- back, you are looking amazing.”

Another comment praised Kelly for seemingly putting herself first following the finalization of her divorce from Brandon Blackstock in 2021. “Kelly I love your new look, working on yourself,” the person wrote.

Then again, there were other comments that disapproved of all of the attention given to Kelly’s weight, saying it shouldn’t matter. “Can we stop boiling down women to their weight? Literally every time a woman loses weight all anyone talks about is how good they look. You aren’t complimenting her. You are letting her know she didn’t look good enough before,” one comment pointed out.

A different one said, “I hate that people are always commenting on other people’s bodies. Even if there’s good intentions. I’m glad she’s healthy and happy.”

There Are Various Theories About Kelly’s Recent Weight Loss

Kelly hasn’t directly commented on her recent weight loss, though there’s been speculation that her Las Vegas residency, Chemistry…an Intimate Night With Kelly Clarkson, has played a part, which began in July 2023. The regular, high-energy shows require a lot of stamina and physical preparation, which could explain the singer’s body transformation.

In the past, Kelly revealed she lost 40 pounds by following the diet outlined in Dr. Steven R. Gundry's book, The Plant Paradox. “It's basically about how we cook our food, non-GMO, no pesticides, eating really organic," Kelly explained in 2018, adding that she began the diet plan for health reasons.

“I literally read this book, and I did it for this autoimmune disease that I had, and I had a thyroid issue, and now all my levels are back up. I'm not on medicine anymore because of this book," the singer continued.

Kelly’s divorce from Brandon extended for years as they tried to divide their assets. The musician ended up having to make a million-dollar payment to her ex to settle the split. Though it’s now over, the singer has been open about how she worries the dramatic divorce has affected her two kids, who now reside with her full-time in Los Angeles.
