C.J. Ham Biography; Contract, House, Height, Weight, Instagram, College

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, August 28, 2024

C.J Ham is a popular American Footballer. He was born on July 22, 1993, in Duluth, Minnesota, United States.

Ham plays as a fullback for the  Minnesota Vikings team of the National Football League (NLF). According to Astrologers, Ham is a Leo by zodiac signs.

C.J. Ham Contract

C.J. Ham signed a four-year contract of $12,000,000 with the Minnesota Vikings. The contract includes a $3,000,000 signing bonus, an average annual salary of $3,000,000 and $5,000,000 guaranteed.

C.J. Ham House

C.J. Ham bought a home in Lakeville, which is about 20 minutes from TCO Performance Center.

C.J. Ham Height

C.J. Ham is 5ft 11In tall.

C.J. Ham Weight

C.J. Ham weighs 236lbs which is 107kg.

C.J. Ham Instagram

C.J. Ham is active on Instagram. He has about 51 thousand followers.

C.J. Ham College

Ham attended Augustana University in Sioux Falls South Dakota from 2011-2015. He was a four-year letter winner in both tracks, football and field events.

As a junior in 2014 Ham played for the second straight season in the 11 games for the entire season. He rushed for 69 yards and two touchdowns against UMary on 27 September 2014.
