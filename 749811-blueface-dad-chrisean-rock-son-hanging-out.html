Bluefaces Dad &amp; Chrisean Rocks Son Spend Time Together

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, August 30, 2024

Blueface won't be winning a Father of the Year award anytime soon, especially considering the way he's been treating his youngest son, Junior. The baby boy is only months old, but already, his parents have publicly put him in multiple compromising situations, from Chrisean Rock's failure to secure him in his carrier properly, to Blue posting NSFW images on Twitter/X. There have been some videos of the "Thotiana" hitmaker spending time bonding with his third-born, but for the most part, he prefers to denounce his paternity online, even claiming a few weeks back that he did a new DNA test proving that he's not the father.

As much as this entire process has been emotionally taxing on Rock, she continues to use it to her advantage. The 23-year-old was going Live on Instagram earlier this week, previewing her shady bars aimed at her ex on Sexyy Red's "Bow Bow Bow (F My Baby Dad)." Between rapping along to that, Chrisean gave some life updates to her followers, including one about her little boy spending time with his paternal grandfather.
