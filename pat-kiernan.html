What Is The Age Of Popular NY1 Reporter Pat Kiernan? Is He Married and Who Is His Wife As of June 20

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, August 17, 2024

Canadian-American journalist Pat Kiernan is perhaps best known for his time with American cable news television channel, NY1. Pat’s career in journalism and news broadcasting started over 3 decades ago. Likewise, his popularity has led him to make cameo appearances in many blockbuster movies.

Pat Kiernan’s age as of June 2020 is 51 years old, he was born on November 20, 1968, in Calgary, Alberta, Canada.

Blissful Marital Relationship with Wife, Dawn Lerohl

Kiernan is a married man, he tied the knot on June 4, 1994 to Dawn Lerohl. Two years after they got married, they moved to the United States.

Their first child, Lucy was born on October 27, 2001 and their second child, Maeve was born on July 12, 2004.

Moving To The USA And Into Expensive Houses

Pat and his wife shifted into a property in Manhattan in 1996, they both have US and Canadian citizenships. Later on, they bought a house in Williamsburg, Brooklyn, USA for a whopping $2.03 million.

He sold a duplex at 171 North 7th Street for $1.095 million before moving out, the pair had originally listed the house for $1.23 million in November 2015, but the house sold for just over a million dollars in 2016.

Career In Film

While not an actor himself, Pat has appeared in various movies and shows mostly as himself. Most notably, he became a part of the MCU and cameoed in movies like The Avengers.

His first appearance in a film was in 2001’s Storytelling. Then, in 2005 he was a part of The Interpreter. Kiernan appeared in two films True North and Night at the Museum.

After a short hiatus from the big screen, he appeared on The Son of No One and 4:44 Last Day on Earth in 2011. The next year, in 2012, he was featured in The Avengers which was the biggest movie of that year.

He continued his affiliation with Marvel when he appeared in Iron Man 3 in 2013. Some of his other popular movies are Spider-Man: Far From Home, Doctor Strange, The Amazing Spider-Man 2, etc.
