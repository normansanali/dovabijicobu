The Star-Studded Cast Of Love Actually Ranked By Net Worth

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, October 1, 2024

Love Actually is a popular holiday film that was released in 2003. It's cast is pretty stellar with big names such as Emma Thompson, Hugh Grant, Liam Neeson, and Colin Firth. It tells the story of love between different couples and has won the hearts of many fans over the years.

Related: The Truth About Casting Love Actually

With such big names in the cast, fans may be wondering how they all rank up according to their net worths now, nearly twenty years after the film was released. Did we forget to mention that Kiera Knightly is also in the cast? Or how about Martin Freeman who was in the UK version of The Office? Let's find out where exactly these huge celebs rank according to how much wealth they have accumulated over the years.

9 Bill Nighy Is Worth $8 Million

Bill Nighy is most known for his role in Love Actually, however he is also well-known for his role as Davy Jones in the Pirates of the Caribbean movies. He also starred in a movie for the BBC called Page Eight, alongside Rachel Weisz, Michael Gambon, and Ralph Fiennes. It is also worth noting that he played a dad in the movie About Time starring Rachel McAdams.

8 Laura Linney Is Worth $10 Million

Laura Linney had the starring role of Cathy Jamison on the series The Big C, which ran for four successful seasons on Showtime. Aside from her role on that show, she is known for her roles in films such as Savages, Kinsey and You Can Count On Me. Linney also starred in the hugely successful Netflix series, Ozark, opposite Jason Bateman, which ran for four seasons.

7 Martine McCutcheon Is Worth $13 Million

Martine McCutcheon is known for her role as Tiffany on the Eastenders series. She is also known as a British singer. She began her music career in the late '90s and early 2000s but put it on hold after her third album didn't do so well in 2002. She returned to music in 2017 with a new album.

6 Martin Freeman Is Worth $20 Million

Freeman played the role of Bilbo in the successful Hobbit films. He is also known for playing Tim in the UK version of The Office. He has also had roles in Marvel films such as Black Panther and Captain America: Civil War. Freeman has also appeared in The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy as well as Hot Fuzz.

5 Colin Firth Is Worth $25 Million

Colin Firth is mostly known for his role in The King's Speech, for which he won an Oscar. He is also known for starring in Bridget Jones' Diary, alongside his Love Actually co-star, Hugh Grant. He also starred in HBOMax's The Staircase and has been in 90 on-screen roles since he began working in 1984. He also starred in the screen adaptation of Mamma Mia! the musical. Firth also had an extensive career in theater in the '80s and '90s in London and the West End.

4 Emma Thompson Is Worth $40 Million

Thompson is known for her role as Professor Trelawney in the Harry Potter films and has nearly one hundred credits under her name on IMDb. She's an incredible actress who has had a tremendous career and has had roles in films such as Saving Mr. Banks, Cruella, and Sense and Sensibility. Thompson, like Firth, has also had a career in theater, which mostly took place in the '80s.

3 Keira Knightly Is Worth $80 Million

Knightly is known for her work in the Pirates of the Caribbean films as well as for her roles in films such as Pride and Prejudice, Atonement, and The Imitation Game. Knightly has been working as an onscreen actress since the early '90s and has nearly sixty credits under her name. While Knightly has done some TV work over the years, most of her career has been in film.

2 Liam Neeson Is Worth $145 Million

Liam Neeson, known for his role in the Taken movies, has also starred in hit movies such as Kinsey, Schindler's List, The Grey and The A-Team. He has acted in over 130 onscreen roles since he began working in 1978. He was in Batman and The Dark Knight Rises and also appeared in Star Wars. Neeson also had a career as a theater actor from the '90s until the early 2000s on Broadway.

1 Hugh Grant Is Worth $150 Million

Hugh Grant takes the cake as the cast member from Love Actually who is worth the most money today. He has seventy acting credits under his name on IMDb, and has starred in hit films such as About a Boy, Four Weddings and a Funeral, Notting Hill, and of course, Love Actually, which is the role he is most known for. He starred in the Bridget Jones' Diary films alongside his Love Actually co-star, Colin Firth, and Nine Months alongside Julianne Moore.
