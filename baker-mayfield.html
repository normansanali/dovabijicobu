Baker Mayfield

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, July 23, 2024

Baker Mayfield, age 25, is an American football quarterback who plays in the National Football League. He was picked by the Cleveland Browns in the 2018 draft.

Baker’s Bio & Family

The talented player was born to parents James Mayfield and Gina Mayfield on April 14, 1995, in Austin, Texas, the United States. His father, James, is a private equity consultant, who also played college football in the past. Baker has an elder brother in the family named Matt Mayfield.

Baker Mayfield with his brother Matt Mayfield on April 17, 2018 (Baker’s Instagram)

Baker attended the Lake Travis High School for his early education where he also kicked-started his amateur football career. Later on, he moved to the University of Oklahoma and played for a college team for three seasons. Baker holds white ethnicity and his birth sign is Aries.

Career & Net Worth

Baker Mayfield was selected by the Cleveland Browns as the first overall pick in the 2018 NFL Draft. Immediately, he penned a four-year rookie contract with the team on July 24, 2018, that included a deal worth $32.68 million in guaranteed salary.

Baker Mayfield was drafted by the Cleveland Browns in 2018 (Baker’s Instagram)

The young player ringed his debut for the Browns in Week 3 on Thursday Night Football against the New York Jets. He went 17 of 23, passing for 201 yards as his team prevailed 21-17 against the Jets. He later finished his 2019 season with 3,827 passing yards, 22 touchdowns, and 21 interceptions from 16 games.

Baker’s Personal Life

The sportsperson is relishing his blissful married life. He walked down the aisle together with his beautiful wife named Emily Wilkinson in October 2019. After dating for a while, the pair shared the engagement rings in 2018, before taking their relationship further to the vows of marriage.

Baker Mayfield married wife Emily Wilkinson in 2019 (Baker’s Instagram)

Since their nuptial bond, the couple keeps on sharing social media moments with one another.

Height & Body Statistics

Baker stands tall at a height of 6 feet and 1 inch (1.85 meters). Besides, he weighs around 215 pounds (98 kg). He has black hair and brown eyes.
