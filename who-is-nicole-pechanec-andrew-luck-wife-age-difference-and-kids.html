Who Is Nicole Pechanec? Learn About The Former Gymnast

Nicole Pechanec is a Czech American former gymnast, who was part of the Czech national team in the World Championship.

Pechanec was born in Newark, New Jersey, the United States to Tomas Pechanec, and Yvette Pechanec. She also has a sister named Monica Pechanec.

Nicole’s mother herself was a former gymnast, which probably had a huge impact on Nicole’s choice to pursue gymnastics. Nicole’s father was also an athlete. He was on the Czech national sailing team, and represent them.

After her Bachelor’s degree in Engineering from Stanford, the former gymnast has also earned an MBA from Kelley School of Business. 

She has also been associated with the non-profit organization, Nest.

The former gymnast currently lives in Indianapolis with her husband and child.

Nicole has been very active with her career after her career in gymnastics as well. She is an entrepreneur, who is always looking to make the world a better place.
