Who is Ashley Graham?

Ashley Graham is an American Model. She has appeared in Levi’s campaign.

Graham is a Plus-size model. She has been covered in the fashion magazines like Vogue, Glamour, Ellie, and Harper’s Bazaar.

NPR interviewed her about plus-size modeling.

Ashley Graham: Birth Facts, Parents, and Childhood

Graham was born on October 30, 1987. She was born in Nebraska, US. She is the daughter of Linda Graham. Her father’s name is still unknown.

Her ethnicity is Mixed (German and English).

Ashley Graham spent most of her childhood in Lincoln Nebraska. Graham has a sister named Abigail Graham.

In which school did Ashley Graham attend?

Graham completed her school in 2002 from Scott Middle school. She joined Lincoln Southwest High School in 2002.

Ashley Graham: Early Professional Life and Career

Ashley Graham was identified by the I&I agency in 2000. After attending a model convention, she signed a deal in 2001 with Wilhelmina Models. She worked with Ford Models in 2003.

Vogue Magazine’s Sally Singer profiled her in April 2007. She appeared in the Glamour of October 2009. The issue was “These Bodies are Beautiful at Every Size.”

In 2010, Graham appeared in a controversial TV commercial for Lane Bryant. Later, to address the controversy she appeared on Jay Leno’s “The Tonight Show” on May 31, 2010.

Ashley Graham appeared in the Bust magazine editorial in December 2010. In 2011, Graham was in several campaigns for Levi’s. She has also done modeling for the Marina Rinaldi Campaign.

In New York, she was featured on the Billboards for Lane Bryant in December 2012.

Graham appeared as a coach for the aspiring plus-size model on MTV. She has designed a lingerie line for the Addition Elle.

Ashley Graham was a model in the international Harper’s Bazaar issue pre-fall collection and beauty editorial in May 2014.  Graham model in an advertisement for the Swimsuit issue in 2015.

Ashley 2016 served as a backstage host for Miss USA 2016, Miss Universe 2016, Miss USA 2017, and Miss Universe 2017.

She is the judge of the VH1 program America’s Next Top Model.  She wrote a book in 2017 named“A New Model: What confidence, beauty, and power look like”.

Awards and Achievements
