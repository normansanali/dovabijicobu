Miranda Lambert Albums

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 3, 2024

Miranda Lambert Albums: Miranda Lambert, officially known as Miranda Leigh Lambert is an American country singer and guitarist born on November 10, 1983.

She developed a love for music at a very younger age and has been consistent throughout her career rising through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after acts within this showbiz space.

At the age of 16, she appeared on the Johnnie High Country Music Revue in Arlington, Texas, the same talent show that had helped launch the career of LeAnn Rimes.

As an independent artist, she released her self-titled debut album in 2001 and in 2003, she finished third place on Nashville Star, a singing competition that aired on the USA Network.

Apart from her solo career, Miranda Lambert is a member of the Pistol Annies formed in 2011 alongside Ashley Monroe and Angaleena Presley.

She has been honored by the Grammy Awards, the Academy of Country Music Awards, and the Country Music Association Awards.

Lambert has been honored with more Academy of Country Music Awards than any artist in history.

As of February 2023, she had released 9 studio albums, and several other singles and has received several awards and accolades with most of her albums being certified Platinum.

Miranda Lambert has nine tattoos of various objects and symbols, and the word “tumbleweed”.

Miranda Lambert Albums

At the time of this writing (February 2023), Miranda Lambert had served the music community with 9 studio albums; Miranda Lambert (2001), Kerosene (2005), Crazy Ex-Girlfriend (2007), Revolution (2009), Four the Record (2011), Platinum (2014), The Weight Of These Wings (2016), Wildcard (2019) and Palomino (2022).
