Ciaras Stunning Mother, Jackie Harris, And Grandmother Gladys Stewart Were The Highlight In Red C

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, September 17, 2024

Diva Ciara looked highly fashionable at the 2019 VH1 Dear Mama: A Love Letter to Mom tribute, but she was no match for her stunning mother, Jackie Harris, and grandmother Gladys Stewart.

Ahead of Mother’s Day, a long list of celebrities and their mothers gathered at the Theater at the Ace Hotel to talk about the women who gave them life and shaped them.

While on the red carpet, Ciara revealed her favorite memories with her mom by saying: “Sharing with my mom that I’m having a baby! That’s pretty sweet. She was in shock. And it’s beautiful, and now I have two babies, and the shock never gets old for my mom. It’s a sweet thing to be able to share with your mother, and my mom was really proud and excited that she [was] going to be a grandma for the first and second time!”

Russell Wilson’s wife also shared the best advice her mother gave her: “The best piece of advice was [about] motherhood. When I was going to enter mommyhood, she was like, ‘Don’t worry. Everything will fall in place.’ That was really comforting to know because you’re so curious about this thing that you’ve never experienced before. It’s very foreign to your mind and your body, and with motherhood that is true, it just falls into place. You don’t need to worry about the process.”
