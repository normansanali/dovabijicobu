Fantasia Barrino Surprises Fans With Picture Of 17-Year-Old Daughter Zion Graduating High School Wit

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, September 16, 2024

Fantasia Barrino is a very proud mother, who watched her 17-year-old daughter, Zion Quari Barrino, graduate from high school with her father, Brandel Shouse.

The talented singer and American Idol winner posted a few pictures of her gorgeous daughter smiling from ear to ear as she posed with both parents.

The teenager wore a beautiful olive dress, silver robe, and matching cap as she hugged her mother and father.

Fantasia wrote this in the caption: “Zion❤️ You changed my Life, you made all things right and at the time when most thought it was the End-for me when I became pregnant. It was really the beginning of a Beautiful Journey and now Look at “you” look at that smile. #proudmom”

Fans are beyond proud of the mother/daughter duo and rightfully so. Fantasia dropped out of school in the ninth grade at 14 and gave birth to her daughter at the age of 17.
