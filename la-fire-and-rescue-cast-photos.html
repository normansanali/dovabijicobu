Meet The Real-Life Chicago Fire Hollywood Life

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, August 12, 2024

Missing your One Chicago Wednesdays? Same. Well, NBC has a treat for you. The new docuseries ‘LA Fire & Rescue’ is now airing this summer. The show gives access to the inner workings of the Los Angeles County Fire Department. This fire department is responsible for protecting the lives and property of four million residents living in 59 cities across Los Angeles County. The LACoFD’s 2,300 square miles of service are unlike any other in the United States. ‘LA Fire & Rescue’ includes the responses to real-life calls.

The firehouses featured this season are Station 8 (West Hollywood), Station 16 (Watts), Station 37 (Palmdale), Station 41 (Compton), Station 101 (Claremont), Station 125 (Calabasas), and Station 172 (Inglewood). The show also features Air Operations and the L.A. County Fire Department Lifeguard Division. The show airs Wednesdays on NBC. See more photos of the ‘LA Fire & Rescue’ cast in our gallery now.
