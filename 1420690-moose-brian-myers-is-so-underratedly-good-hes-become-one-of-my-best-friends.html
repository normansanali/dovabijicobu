Moose: Brian Myers Is So Underratedly Good, Hes Become One Of My Best Friends

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, July 27, 2024

Moose has high praise for fellow IMPACT Wrestling star Brian Myers.

IMPACT/TNA Wrestling star Moose recently sat down with WrestleZone’s Managing Editor Bill Pritchard. When asked about his relationship with Brian Myers and getting his own action figure in 2024, Moose spoke highly of Myers and said he doesn’t get the credit he deserves.

“We’re working on that,” Moose said. “I do have one action figure, which he gifted me, and it’s of my favorite athlete of all time, Russell Westbrook. That’s the only action figure I have. But working with Brian Myers has been a pleasure. Brian Myers is so underratedly good, and he doesn’t get the credit he should for how good of a performer he is.

“I’m so happy with the last few months that we’ve become really good friends. He’s probably one of my best friends, and I’ve been a sponge learning a lot from him because there’s a lot of things that he does in the ring that I’ve never even thought of that I could add to my game. So just working with him the last few months has been a pleasure of mine. Just right there, we made a bond and a friendship that will last forever.”
