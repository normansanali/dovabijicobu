Tyson Fury: what is the boxer's net worth? Get the details

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

Tyson Fury recently welcomed his seventh child with his wife Paris, but how much do you know about the boxing legend? Find out more about his career and amazing wealth here... 

Who is Tyson Fury?

Tyson hails from Manchester, and is best known for his success in the boxing ring, winning Fighter of the Year in 2015 after winning several heavyweight titles. The professional boxer - famously dubbed the 'Gypsy King' - has held the World Boxing Council heavyweight title since 2020. 

He wed Paris in 2008, and together they share seven children: Venezuela, Prince John James, Prince Tyson II, Valencia Amber, Prince Adonis Amaziah, Athena, and a baby boy whose name is yet to be revealed. 

What is Tyson's net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, his net worth currently stands at nearly £50million ($65 million), and he has certainly made the most of it! The star owns a mansion in Morecambe, although his wife Paris recently revealed that the planned to move.

After defeating Derek Chisora and Dillian Whyte, Tyson reportedly took home an eye-watering £45 million. Meanwhile, in 2022, the father-of-seven allegedly pocketed a cool $60 million after a rematch with Wilder and a subsequent fight against Dillian Whyte. 

Chatting on Loose Women, she explained: "We get people around the house. It's not good, we actually have to because of that. We're in the process of moving house because we've had too many people at the door - too many strangers knocking.

"And we have five children here, it's not like me and Tyson's here, two adults who can sort of deal with situations. We have people coming to the house and the kids are opening the door and we don't know who they're opening the door to. It's not what we need. It's not the ideal situation."

Tyson also has an incredible car collection, including a £280,000 Rolls-Royce, a £166,000 Ferrari Porotfino and a £38,000 Land Rover Defender. He previously revealed that his son, Prince, admitted to accidentally spilling a can of coke in the Land Rover. 

At the time, he told the Lockdown Lowdown: "It's one of those Land Rover Defenders so it's got a rubber floor so it didn't really matter. But I was teaching him a lesson. He thought it was a really bad thing he had done but he owned up to it and he told the truth. 

"And he knows that if he tells me the truth about anything he does that is naughty he will not get in trouble. But if he lies to me, then he gets in trouble." 

Tyson's endorsements and Netflix show

Beyond his boxing career, Tyson has made a small fortune from a plethora of brand deals. He has his own line of Furiocity energy drinks, a selection of bestselling books and now a hit TV show on Netflix called At Home with the Furys.

On his own website, meanwhile, the boxer sells a selection of T-shirts, gym vests, memorabilia and signed goods.
