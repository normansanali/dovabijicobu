Michael J. Fox Latest News and Updates

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, July 19, 2024

Known to many as beloved Marty Mcfly from the Back to the Future franchise, Canadian actor Michael J. Fox has been in the acting industry for over 40 years now. Getting his big break on the show Family Ties (1982), he went on to star in his first movie — High School U.S.A (1983). After that, he solidified his career with his popular roles in both Back to the Future (1985) and the original Teen Wolf (1985).

Today, he is living life a little differently as he continues his battle with Parkinson's disease. He has one son and three daughters who he shares with his partner of over 35 years Tracy Pollan. His kids are Sam Michael Fox, Aquinnah Kathleen Fox, Schuyler Frances Fox, and Esmé Annabelle Fox.

Full Name: Michael Andrew Fox

Birthday: June 9, 1961

Relationship Status: Married to Tracy Pollan

Instagram: @realmikejfox

Twitter: @realmikefox

Net Worth: $65 million

Fun Fact: He added the "J" in his name to pay homage to actor Michael J. Pollard.
