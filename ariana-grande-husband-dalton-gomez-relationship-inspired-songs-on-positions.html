These Songs On Ariana Grande's 'Positions' Reveal The Truth About Her Relationship With Husband Dalt

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, July 20, 2024

Ariana Grande has been open about the fact that her husband, Dalton Gomez, was a source of inspiration for her 'Positions' album. Ariana Grande's 'Positions' is her sixth studio album, released on October 30, 2020. The album is a mix of pop, R&B, and trap, and features collaborations with different artists. The album debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 chart and received multiple nominations.

In an interview with Apple Music, Grande stated that the album was written during the early stages of her relationship with Gomez and that his presence in her life influenced her songwriting. Grande also revealed that Gomez was present during the creation of the album and helped her to narrow down the tracklist. We've taken the liberty of rounding up the most heartwarming and adorable tracks from the album, dedicated to her husband Dalton Gomez.

8 Ariana Grande Sings About The Start Of Her Relationship With Dalton Gomez In 'Off The Table'

Ariana Grande collaborates with The Weeknd once again on the song 'Off the Table'. In this track, Grande explores her doubts and anxieties about love, expressing fears that she may not be ready to fully commit to a new relationship after experiencing past heartbreak.

However, the lyrics also reveal that Grande has found a deep and meaningful connection with her husband. Similarly, on the track 'Six thirty', Grande ponders the possibility of a long-term relationship with Gomez, expressing a desire to build a lasting partnership.

7 Dalton Gomez Helped Ariana Grande Move On From Her Previous Heartbreaks in 'Safety Net'

In ‘Safety Net’, Ariana Grande explores her conflicting emotions following her previous relationships. The lyrics of the song suggest that Grande was not expecting to find love again so soon and that she is struggling with mixed feelings about her new partner. On one hand, she desires to trust her heart and be vulnerable in her new relationship. On the other hand, she is hesitant to let her guard down and wants to maintain a sense of emotional detachment. 6 Ariana Grande Makes A Lot Of References On How Calm Dalton Gomez Keeps Her In 'Love Language'

Ariana Grande reflects on her past relationships and seems to have reached a point of acceptance in ‘Love Language’. With the introduction of Dalton into her life, she appears to have adopted a more optimistic outlook.

The song seems to emphasize the importance of communication and understanding in a relationship, as Grande sings about learning to speak her partner's "love language" to deepen their connection.

5 Ariana Grande Doesn't Want Mixed Signals In The Song 'Motive'

In this track, Ariana sings about the strong sexual attraction that existed between herself and her husband Dalton Gomez from the outset of their relationship. However, Grande also expresses her desire to know if Gomez was interested in her for more than just physical intimacy.

4 Ariana Grande Gets Cheeky With The Song '34+35'

Ariana's second single ‘34+35’ features explicit lyrics that celebrate her sexual chemistry with Dalton Gomez. The song showcases a more risqué side of Grande, as she sings about their bedroom antics and making the bed shake. It became an instant hit and a TikTok dance craze.

A similar vibe can be found in the track "Nasty," which features suggestive lyrics and a seductive melody. In "My Hair," Grande takes a more intimate approach, using the song to serenade her partner and express her affection.

3 'West Side' Reveals Ariana Grande’s Desire For A Future With Dalton

The song ‘West Side’ is a vulnerable and intimate track in which Grande expresses her hopes and desires for her future with Dalton Gomez. The lyrics of the song convey a sense of deep love and commitment, with Grande singing about wanting to be a part of Gomez's life in a permanent way.

She also promises to be a source of light and vitality for him, suggesting that she sees herself as a supportive and uplifting partner.

1 'POV' is Ariana Grande’s Love Letter To Dalton Gomez

The final track on the album is a poignant and emotional tribute to her husband Dalton Gomez. The lyrics of the song convey a deep sense of love and intimacy, with Grande singing about wanting to see herself through Gomez's eyes and to feel the same level of unconditional love that he offers her.

The song is a powerful testament to the strength and depth of their connection and serves as a beautiful conclusion to the album. Fans of Grande have been moved by the song's heartfelt lyrics, with many expressing their admiration and appreciation for the love that she shares with Gomez as it shows just how close Ariana and Dalton have become in less than a year.
