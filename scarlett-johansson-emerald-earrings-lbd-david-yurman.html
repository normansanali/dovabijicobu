Scarlett Johansson Accents Her Elegant Holiday Look with Gorgeous Emerald Earrings at David Yurman E

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, July 14, 2024

Scarlett Johansson celebrated her ambassadorship with David Yurman in a classy little black dress perfect for a holiday soiree.

Noam Galai

Johansson, who became the luxury jewelry brand’s first celebrity spokesperson in 2022, attended an event at the brand’s flagship store in Midtown Manhattan on Wednesday. She looked breathtakingly elegant in a black minidress that featured an off-the-shoulder design and puffy white sleeves complete with bow details. She paired the lovely piece with black pointed-toe mules.

Noam Galai

The Black Widow actor pulled her blonde hair back into a sleek low bun with a sweet black ribbon bow—which revealed something gorgeous and sparkling. Johansson made a statement with diamond-encrusted drop earrings that featured a massive emerald gem. She kept the rest of her accessories minimal but still luxurious, wearing gold and diamond rings, and glammed up with black winged eyeliner, a glossy nude lip, and shiny dark red nails.

John Lamparski

Johansson stepped out onto the red carpet at the American Museum of Natural History’s 2023 gala earlier this month sporting another LBD—this time, she walked arm-in-arm with Colin Jost, her husband of three years. The two looked so in love while wearing coordinating black-and-white outfits on their rare public date night.

Johansson layered a boxy, floor-length coat in an icy white hue over a black slip dress and added pops of red with a vibrant pocket square and bright lipstick. She accented her winter look with black pointed-toe heels, a matching croc-embossed clutch, and a diamond tennis necklace. The Saturday Night Live star looked dapper in a classic black tux and shiny dress shoes.
