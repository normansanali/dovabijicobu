Who Is Kathryne Padgett? Her Bio, Age, Job, Height, Alex Rodriguez

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Kathryne Padgett was a well-known name in the Dallas-based fitness competitive community. But in 2022, she shot to the international limelight not because of her profession but rather for vacationing out with the former professional baseball shortstop/businessman Alex Rodriguez.

This Kathryne Padgett Bio explores her life.

Alex Rodriguez And Kathryne Padgett Dating?

No, Kathryne Padgett and Alex Rodriguez weren’t dating.

“A-Rod is single and enjoying life,” an insider shed light on their alleged relationship.“It’s not like she’s his girlfriend. They went to Green Bay and then to Minnesota. She’s not his girlfriend. He is a single dude,” he added.

Even Kathryne’s FB labeled her to be “single” then.

Kathryne, first made headlines on Jan 22, 2022, after she was spotted freezing in the stands of Lambeau Field with the baseball star. It seems that the duo chose the  freezing weather instead of cozying of in a heated room because Alex “wanted to experience Lambeau Field in the stands.”

However, that wasn’t their first sighting.

The pair was previously spotted on the 50-yard line at the Packers game, both wearing the yellow Packers hats. Later that week, the duo then made it to the Minneapolis Target Center to watch the Timberwolves take on the Brooklyn Nets. That night, Kathryne even took it to her IG to post pics from the court flaunting black leggings, and a black leather jacket.

Apart from the sightings, Kathryne and Alex also have reportedly vacationed in Miami. What more confirms this rumor is Kathryne’s IG pic from the Florida city taken on a boat that looks similar to Rodriguez’s.

But this might just be one of Alex’s flings. After the star’s split with Jennifer Lopez in April 2021, he was also spotted with reporter Melanie Collins vacationing in St. Tropez and Ibiza, Spain.

Also, there were rumors that Alex was linked to the Southern Charm star Madison LeCroy.

Alex has dated many high-profile celebrities over the years. Here’s a list:

Kathryne Padgett Age

Kathryne Padgett was born in 1996. That made her 25 years of age in Jan 2022.

She is 16 years younger than Alex, who was born on July 27, 1975.

Kathryne Padgett Job

Kathryne Padgett is an NPC (National Physique Committee) competitor who’s won two titles in the NPC competition that was held in 2021. But when she wasn’t competing, she worked as a director of nutrition at Doyle Sports Performance.

“Doyle Sports Performance” is a level training facility based in Hiram, GA, offering training sessions for athletes to increase speed, power, and agility for all sports.

Also, Kathryne is an aspiring Registered Dietitian. Someday she wants to pursue RD as her full-time job.

As for her education, Kathryne attended the University of St. Thomas in Texas and graduated with a 9.9 GPA in biology/pre-dental major in 2019. During her time there, Kathryne was also actively involved with its soccer team. No wonder, she was presented with the student-athlete award for the women’s soccer team after playing  72 games for the Celts.

Talking more about her involvement with soccer, Kathryne played the mid position for the UST Celts and never lost a match in her senior year. Yes, they dominated the field, with once a win of 12-0 against Jarvis Christian.

Kathryne Padgett Family

Kathryne Padgett comes from a family of five. Her parents are named, James and Stephanie Padgett, and her two siblings are Kathryne and Grayson.

Here’s a little info on them:

James: A Bevil Oaks, Texas native, who worked as an attorney for James M. Padgett, Attorney at Law.

Stephanie: She worked at Hartmann Building Specialties, Ltd.

Raegan: Graduated from Hardin-Jefferson High School, and went on to study Doctor of Physical Therapy at the Baylor University

Grayson: A baseball player from Houston

Also, it seems like all the Padgett siblings were athletes. What’s more, Kathryne and her sister Stephanie were even considered two of the top soccer players in Southeast Texas. Reportedly, they were also the faces of the Hardin-Jefferson girls’ soccer program for three seasons.

Trivia: Raegan got engaged to the American professional baseball catcher Shea Langeliers in Jan 2022. The two were scheduled to hold their wedding on January 21, 2023.
