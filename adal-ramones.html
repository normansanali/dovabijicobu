Who is Adal Ramones?

Adal Ramones is a well-known TV show host who was born in Mexico on December 3, 1961. This prominent television personality and comedian is best known for anchoring the Mexican variety show Otro Rollo. He also hosted the reality series Bailando por un Sueo (Dancing for a Dream) and its sequel, Cantando por un Sueo (Singing for a Dream). Adal Ramones’ zodiac sign is Sagittarius, according to astrologers.

Adalberto Javier Ramones Martnez (born December 3, 1961) is a Mexican comedian and television host who is well-known for his observations on Mexican and international social issues. Ramones was the host of Otro Rollo, a popular Mexican television show produced by Televisa and shown in 53 countries, including the United States, where it was broadcast by Univision. With Gaby Valencia, he has a daughter named Paola and a son named Diego.

Ramones was kidnapped in 1998, only a month before his wedding. His kidnappers blindfolded him and shackled his wrists and feet in a cupboard. During those seven days, he lost 15.5 pounds since he was only allowed to drink the bare minimum of water and was continually threatened by the criminal gang. This kidnapping story is covered in three chapters of his autobiography, which will be published by the “Planeta” publishing house.
