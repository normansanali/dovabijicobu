Travis Kelce Continues To Come Clean About If He Actually Likes Taylor Swift's Music

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Highlights

  • Kelce is a big fan of Taylor Swift's music, appreciating her work and a specific era of her career.
  • Kelce is a fan of Swift's concerts, attending multiple shows and praising the experience.
  • Travis Kelce's respect for Taylor Swift as a musician has grown, with him being supportive and proud of her journey.

When Taylor Swift's most passionate fans talk about her music, it quickly becomes clear how much her songs mean to them. For that reason, millions of people want to know how many songs Swift has written and what her most popular songs are.

When Swift and Travis Kelce became a couple, fans initially wanted to know things like if they would build a family and what his exes thought of him. Unfortunately, fans also wondered how Kelce would handle the pressure of dating Swift. Over time, however, some of Swift's fans became most curious if Kelce was a fan of Swift's music.

This article will reveal what Travis Kelce reportedly thinks about Taylor Swift's music. Then, the article will look at how Kelce feels about Taylor Swift's concerts.

Travis Kelce Seems To Be A Big Fan Of Taylor Swift's Music

Since Taylor Swift is one of the biggest music stars of all time, it is no surprise when a celebrity reveals they like their music. However, after Swift and Travis Kelce became a couple, fans were left wondering if he liked her music.

In May 2024, Kelce held his "Kelce Jam" Music Festival. While attending that event, Kelce spoke to Access Hollywood. An article from The Daily Mail revealed that Access Hollywood asked Kelce about Swift's music.

Based on the resulting conversation, it seemed very clear that Kelce truly appreciates Swift's music.

If the outlet asked Kelce if he liked Swift's music in general, it would have been fascinating to see his answer. After all, Kelce simply saying that he loved Swift's music, or it wasn't for him would be very revealing.

Kelce went on to pick his favorite era of Swift's career without missing a beat. Then, Kelce kept going by admitting he was biased to love the most recent era of Swfit's career.

"Oh I'm a big 1989 era [fan]. But I'm not gonna lie, I might be a little biased toward The Tortured Poets Department, just a little bit."

In response, Access Hollywood's reporter asked a follow-up question about which song Kelce would have Swift perform at his music festival. After taking a second to consider that question, Kelce replied, "'So High School' might be the only one that's just jumping into my mind right now, I think everybody would get fired up for that one."

On another occasion in April 2024, Kelce proved how much he respects Swift as a musician. As an ABC News article revealed, Kelce talked to The Hollywood Reporter about how talented he thought Swift was as a performer.

"She's on a whole other stratosphere. She's the best at what she does for a reason. It's because she's so articulate and just very dialed into every single thing that she does."

Also in April 2024, Elle published an article that cited Entertainment Tonight's report after they spoke to a Swift-Kelce insider. According to that insider, Kelce is extremely supportive of Swift's music career. The insider also stated that Kelce has become a fan of Swift's music and that he is proud to be part of her journey.

“Travis is so supportive of the entire album and loves that he is a part of Taylor’s story. He is a Swiftie through and through, and is very proud of her.”

The same insider went on to say that Kelce believed that Swift was, "extremely talented" and that he admired her, "creative process".

ABC News' aforementioned article also cited a time that Kelce personally spoke to Entertainment Tonight.

According to Travis Kelce, he isn't the only member of his family who has become a big fan of Taylor Swift's music. While speaking with Access Hollywood in May 2024, Kelce revealed that his niece Wyatt's favorite Swift song is "The Man".

During that conversation, the football star told ET that he has so much respect for Swift that she has changed the way he perceives music.

"It's definitely been fun to experience her taste in music, for sure. She's so amazing at what she does. And to find that creativity to see where she likes to pull things from and just, really, how she listens to music is very eye-opening for me. It's been fun to hear her take on it."

Travis Kelce Loves Seeing Taylor Swift's Concerts

As anyone who has followed Taylor Swift's career will already know, her concert tours are huge events. After all, Swift's fans are willing to pay staggering amounts to see her perform live but there is so much demand that many of them still miss out.

Fortunately for Travis Kelce, he has a good connection to get tickets to Swift's concerts and he seems to have taken advantage of that. After all, a May 2024 report from US Weekly revealed that Kelce had attended many of Swift's concerts.

US Weekly's article states that the first time it was revealed that Kelce attended one of Swift's concerts was in July 2023. After that, Kelce watched Swift live in December 2023, as well as in February, March, and April of 2024.

Then in May 2024, Kelce proved once again how great he thinks Swift's concerts are. While talking to US Weekly, Kelce told the outlet that Swift's concerts are "unbelievable" and he stated his opinion that everyone has to see her perform.

“Taylor’s shows are unbelievable. If you haven’t been to them, You got to try it.”
