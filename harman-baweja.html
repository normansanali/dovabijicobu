Harman Baweja is an Indian actor, film producer, and entrepreneur who is known for his work in the Hindi language film industry. A few of his best known roles have been in Love Story 2050 and What’s Your Raashee?.

Education

Harman studied at the Kishore Namit Kapoor Acting Institute in Bombay. He also attended Jamnabai Narsee School.

According to sources, Harman enrolled at the Narsee Monjee College of Commerce and Economics in the past. Later, he went on to attend the University of California, Los Angeles.

    Harman Baweja Facts

  • He was born and raised in India.
  • Harman changed his name to “Harman S Baweja” in 2009, as a tribute to his grandfather’s name.
  • He made his debut theatrical film appearance as Karan Malhotra in Love Story 2050 in 2008, alongside the likes of actress Priyanka Chopra Jonas and Boman Irani.
  • He is often said to have a striking facial resemblance to actor Hrithik Roshan.
  • Harman was featured on the hit TV series of Comedy Nights with Kapil.
  • After his last film appearance as Yogesh Patel in What’s Your Raashee? in 2009, Harman went on a hiatus for almost 5 years before rejoining the Indian cinema with his portrayal of Viki Kartoos in Dishkiyaoon.
