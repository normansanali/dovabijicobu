WWE Stockton Live Event Results (8/6): Ambrose vs Styles for the WWE Title, Orton vs Wyatt, Footage

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 22, 2024

WWE Stockton Live Event Results
8/6/16
Stockton, CA
Results courtesy of PWInsider.com

Top Pops:

1 Cesaro (combo of him and first star the fans saw)
2 Orton
3 Ambrose

1 – Cesaro def. Sheamus.

2 – Jey Uso (No Jimmy at all) def. Fandango after failed interference from Tyler Breeze.

3 – Intercontinental Title Match – The Miz def. Titus O’Neil & Darren Young in a Triple Threat Match.

4 – Sami Zayn def. Baron Corbin.

5 – Tag Team Title Match – New Day (Xavier Woods & Kofi Kingston) def. Karl Anderson & Luke Gallows — Big E came out late in the match – distracted Anderson (who was pinned).

6 – Roman Reigns def. Seth Rollins — Rollins got on the mic and called everyone losers – then turned into a spear from Reigns for the pin. 

7 – Becky Lynch & Carmella def. Natalya & Alexa Bliss (Bliss tapped out to Lynch) Short promo by Eva Marie before the match – mostly to get her introduction over.

8 – Randy Orton def. Bray Wyatt with the RKO.

9 – Dean Ambrose def. AJ Styles to retain the WWE title.
