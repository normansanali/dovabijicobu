Alejandro Fernández Biography

Alejandro Fernández is one of the most popular and richest World Music Singer who was born on April 24, 1971 in Guadalajara, Mexico. Latin pop singer known by the nickname El Potrillo, or The Colt, who has sold over thirty million albums over the course of his career. He is the son of famed singer Vicente Fernández.

He recorded the 1998 album Christmas in Vienna with Plácido Domingo.

He has five children, Camila, America, Valentina, Emiliano and Alex. He was married to America Guinart from 1992 to 1998 and Ximena Diaz from 1998 to 2004.
