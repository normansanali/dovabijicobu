Is well read hyphenated

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, September 28, 2024

Should well written be hyphenated?

If the term “well written” precedes the noun, it is a compound adjective and requires a comma: “It is a well-written essay.” However, when it comes after the noun, no hyphen: “The essay was well written.”

Is well understood hyphenated?

For example, a “well understood concept” is not hyphenated (well is an adverb modifying the adjective understood).

Do you hyphenate well received?

You use a hyphen when the adjective comes before the noun, not after it: It was a well-received show.

How do you use well-read in a sentence?

He is well-read and spends much of his time lecturing to the reader about history, culture, politics, religion and literature. He is extremely intelligent and well-read with an encyclopaedic knowledge on a wide number of subjects. Jamil was extremely cultured and well-read on literature, poetry and politics.

Is well functioning hyphenated?

Include a hyphen if it makes your meaning clearer; otherwise omit it. … There is no hyphen when well functions as an adverb (usually following the noun being modified and a linking verb, such as to be), or when it is qualified (as in extremely well groomed).

Is well ventilated hyphenated?

In the first and third examples, where well comes after the noun it modifies ( i.e. astronaut, studio), a hyphen is not needed because well functions as an adverb: is well known, was well ventilated.

What do you mean by well read?

Definition of well-read

: well-informed or deeply versed through reading well-read in history.

Is well-informed?

If you say that someone is well-informed, you mean that they know a lot about many different subjects or about one particular subject.

What is well written?

Well-written means that when you tell a story, make a point, tell a joke, etc., the writing flows quickly, smoothly, and with little effort on the part of the reader. The reader should find the prose clear and concise, without having to slog through 100 words when ten would have sufficed.
