Ronnie Ortiz-Magro Latest News and Updates

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, July 9, 2024

Television personality Ronnie Ortiz-Magro is best known for being one of the original cast members of the MTV reality series Jersey Shore.

Ronnie worked in real estate before joining the show, which premiere in 2009. Jersey Shore ran for six seasons before ending in 2012. The show was rebooted in 2018 as Jersey Shore: Family Vacation, and Ronnie appeared on the show until 2021, when he took a step back to focus on his mental health. Along with Jersey Shore, Ronnie has appeared on several other reality shows including Famously Single, Celebrity Fear Factor and Hollywood Medium.

After breaking up with longtime girlfriend Sammi "Sweetheart" Giancola in 2016, Ron welcomed a baby girl with then-girlfriend Jen Harley in 2018. Unfortunately, the couple split in 2019 after a physical altercation resulted in Ronnie getting arrested and charged with felony domestic violence. In 2021, Ronnie announced his engagement to Saffire Matos.
