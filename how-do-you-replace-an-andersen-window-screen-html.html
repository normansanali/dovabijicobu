How do you replace an Andersen window screen?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, August 12, 2024

Answer

Open your Andersen window to the maximum extent possible.

Grasp the bottom edge of the screen and raise it up.

Insert the tip of a utility knife into the groove and carefully pry off the thin, black spline.

Remove the old screen from the window and put the window aside for a moment to think.

If required, flip the window over so that the groove side is facing up on the inside.
