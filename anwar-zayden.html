Anwar Zayden Wiki, Age, Height, Weight, Wife, Net worth, Biography &amp; Family

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, September 27, 2024

Anwar Zayden (born October 08, 1961) is an American Actor, Former Bull Rider, Businessman, and Social Media Personality from South Beach, Florida. He garnered immense popularity after portraying one of the recurring roles in the TV series called “Miami Vice” created by Anthony Yerkovich.

It is an American crime drama television program aired on NBC. As per the source, the show ran from September 16, 1984, to January 25, 1990. Furthermore, Anwar portrayed the character named Mendez who was a drug dealer working in Miami originally from Bolivia.

The actor made an appearance in the seventh episode titled “El Viejo” which was shown in the Miami Vice Season 3. In fact, the episode came out on November 7, 1986. Later, Mendez got killed in a gunfight with Jake Pierson (played by Willie Nelson).

Some of the other main casts of Miami Vice are Ricardo Tubbs as Philip Michael Thomas, James “Sonny” Crockett as Don Johnson, Gina Navarro Calabrese as Saundra Santiago, Trudy Joplin as Olivia Brown, etc.

Early Life, Parents, Siblings & Bio

Anwar Zayden’s age is 58 years old, as in 2019. Miami Vice famed actor took birth back in 1961 in South Beach, Florida, the United States of America. His birthday falls on the 8th of October.

Anwar’s parents were Lebanese immigrants who were living in pre-Fidel Cuba. His father “Alfredo Zayden” was an owner of an import/export business. On the other hand, the actor’s mother “Graciella Zayden” remained a homemaker.

He has five siblings i.e. four sisters and one brother. However, their names are not available on the internet. Anwar’s maternal grandfather was a construction magnate.

In 1958, his dad faced tragedy after militants burned Alfredo’s store. Later, they decided to move to the United States of America. After Anwar Zayden was born, Alfredo began to serve the Central Intelligence Agency.

The family moved from one country to another due to Alfredo’s job. They lived in places such as Venezuela, Spain, Puerto Rico, and Lebanon. Zayden was raised in cities like Barcelona and Madrid.

As of education, he was sent to a U.S. Air Force base for the school. At the age of 18, Anwar moved back to Miami, the USA, and started to live with his maternal aunt & uncle.

Dating History, Girlfriend & Spouse

In the initial days of his career, the actor launched a love life with his ex-lover “Yolanda Hadid” (Dutch-American television personality and former model). During an interview, Anwar also said that she was his first love.

The pair remained together for almost seven years. In September 1997, the actor spotted a model named Nancy “Coco” Johnsen at China Grill restaurant. They later connected with each other for breakfast at the News Cafe on Ocean Drive.

However, Johnsen went to the Miami Beach Police and claimed that Anwar had raped her. The actor claimed that the sex was consensual. But he was arrested and charged with rape & kidnapping. He even stayed in jail for 12 days.

Later, the witnesses came forward in order to support Zayden and the charges were dropped. He also filed a wrongful arrest lawsuit in federal court against the department which was settled in 2004.

Back in April 2014, Anwar Zayden was in a romantic relationship with his girlfriend, Anna Volotchko. She was 26 years old at that time and originally belongs to Russia.

In addition, the couple was spotted together at Key Biscayne along with Anna’s mother Elena. Both the ladies were wearing bikinis and dancing with the actor. There are no details on the internet whether the actor is married or not.
