Justin Moldow Biography: Who Is Kaitlin Bennetts Husband?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

Justin Moldow is professionally known as the co-founder of a self-proclaimed libertarian media outlet, Liberty Hangout. He attained stardom as a result of being the husband of an American gun rights activist Kaitlin Bennett.

He has the founder of Young American for Liberty and the President of the Entrepreneurship Club and has been honoured with the Honors Society of Epsilon Sigma Pi and Mu Kappa Tau National Marketing Honors Society.

Justin Moldow biography

You might be wondering who exactly is Justin Moldow and where does he come from? The husband of Kaitlin Bennett was born on June 26th, 1994 in Cleveland, Ohio, United States of America.

He holds American nationality and his ethnicity is mixed as he has Italian and Spanish descent. Individuals are deeply embedded in their religious beliefs and Justin Moldow subscribes to the Christianity doctrine.

After Justin completed his high school education he joined Manhattan College and then graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Marketing.

Justin Moldow career

Justin Moldow and his wife embarked on the journey of founding a libertarian media outlet, Young Jeffersonians on March 5th, 2015 and they later changed it to Liberty Hangout. Kaitlin Bennett is among the content producers of the site and also the face of Liberty Hangout.

Justin Moldow stays behind the scenes while supporting his wife to share their opinions at the forefront of the political commentary. He is also a promoter of Australian economics and property rights. Justin Moldow and Kaitlin Bennett launched a YouTube channel which now has over 631k followers at the time of this publication.

Justin Moldow’s net worth

Justin Moldow has earned it from his political activism and YouTube channel. He has an estimated net worth of $2 million however the information is not official.

Justin Moldow and Kaitlin Bennett

Justin Moldow and his wife, Kaitlin Bennett have been married for a while now as he got engaged on February 4th, 2019 and officially tied the knot on March 19th, 2020 amid the COVID-19 pandemic.

The couple has their wedding celebration in Saint Augustine and they’re now blessed with a child Ravioli.
