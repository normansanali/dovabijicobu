Meghan Markle Embodies Elegance in a Beige Turtleneck Dress

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, July 30, 2024

Duchess Meghan made a rare appearance with husband Prince Harry in a candid video released today by their latest humanitarian initiative.

In the clip, Meghan looks lovely in a beige sleeveless turtleneck dress by sustainable California brand Bleusalt. The subtly sparkly midi features a fold at the waist, separating the top from the skirt.

Meghan styled the elegant dress with gold hoop earrings and gold bangles for her appearance with Harry. For glam, she wore a square cream manicure, natural rosy makeup, and a wavy side-parted hairdo.

Meghan stays mostly out of the spotlight—unless it’s to highlight her and Harry’s projects and charity work—these days, but has been seen out various times this year around her home in Montecito, California, always looking fantastic in sophisticated ensembles.

Last month, she was spotted doing some low-key shopping in at a farmers’ market in the town, accompanied by one of her and Harry’s three dogs. For the occasion, the duchess looked effortlessly chic in her quiet luxury getup: a slinky sand-colored jumpsuit paired with a white linen button-down, a large raffia tote bag, and black aviator sunglasses with golden frames. Her hair was styled in a slicked-back bun, and she seemed to wear natural makeup.

Days earlier, she was seen out with Harry in Santa Barbara. They both wore preppy summer looks, with Meghan opting for a breezy white button-down, which she tucked into brown linen shorts by Reformation. She completed the look with a pair of tan sandals, a white fedora by Janessa Leoné, a gold necklace with a circle pendant, layered bracelets, and oversize black shades. Harry, meanwhile, wore a gray short-sleeved polo shirt with blue jeans, silver-rimmed sunglasses, and a faded blue cap.
