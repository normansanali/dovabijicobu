Are Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott Dating?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, September 4, 2024

Has Kylie Jenner moved onto a new man? The reality star and beauty mogul was spotted at a basketball game last night with Travis Scott, fueling dating rumors between her and the rapper.

Jenner, 19, and Scott, 24, sat together courtside at a Houston Rockets v. Oklahoma City Thunder playoff game in Houston, TX, the rapper's hometown. Some bystanders told People that the two "definitely looked like a couple." They were also seen cuddling up together and Jenner was reportedly kissing Scott's neck.

Getty Images

The pair first sparked romance rumors two weeks ago during Coachella's opening weekend. They were spotted at a party hosted by Jeremy Scott, where they hung out with friends at a VIP table and reportedly left together.

Jenner and her longtime boyfriend Tyga are said to be currently on a break. (He was also seen with a mystery woman last week.) But her fling with Travis Scott might only be about the mind games.

A source told People: “[Scott] and Kylie have also been friends for a while and they all hang out. When she and Tyga are having drama, she tries to make him jealous by hanging out with other guys.”

Interesting.
