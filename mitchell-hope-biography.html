Who is Mitchell Hope?

Mitchell Hope is an Australian actor and model.

In 2015, he starred as Ben in the Disney Channel Original Movies ‘Descendants’ and ‘Descendants 2’.

Mitchell Hope: Professional Life and Career

Hope commenced his acting career in his native Australia in 2006.
