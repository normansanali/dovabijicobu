Was Lance Reddick married? What was Lance Reddicks marriage location?

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 15, 2024

Lance Reddick was married and had a wife. He had been married to Stephanie Reddick since 2011 before he died. Lance Reddick’s marriage location is Loring Park, Minneapolis, Minnesota, United States.

Lance Reddick was an American actor and musician best known for playing iconic roles like Cedric Daniels in The Wire, Phillip Broyles in Fringe, and Chief Irvin Irving in Bosch.

Lance Reddick was born on December 1962, in Baltimore, Maryland, United States 0f America. Lance Reddick attended Friends School of Baltimore and later studied music at the Peabody Preparatory Institute.

Lance Reddick studied classical music composition at the University of Rochester’s Eastman School of Music, where he earned a Bachelor of Music degree.

Lance Reddick voiced the character Commander Zavala in the 2014 and 2017 video games Destiny and Destiny 2, respectively.

In 2022, Lance Reddick portrayed Albert Wesker in the live-action Resident Evil series on Netflix. In 2014, Lance Reddick took on the role of Chief Irvin Irving in the Amazon Prime series Bosch.
