Chloe is a South Korean singer, musician, dancer, and performer who is known as a member of the girl group Cignature. Formed by C9 Entertainment, the group debuted on February 4, 2020, with the debut single Nun Nu Nan Na. Cignature‘s discography also consists of various extended plays and tracks such as My Diary, My Little Aurora, Boyfriend, Listen and Speak, Climax, Villain, Dear Diary Moment, Arisong, Assa, Sirius, I’m Okay, and Aurora.

Chloe Facts

  • She previously trained under Starship Entertainment.
  • Chloe’s Cignature groupmates include Chaesol, Jeewon, Seline, Belle, Semi, and Dohee.
  • Cignature released its debut extended play titled Listen and Speak on September 22, 2020, and its tracklist included Nun Nu Nan Na, Daldalhae, Assa, HingHing, and Arisong. The EP peaked at #21 on the Gaon Album Chart.
  • On June 14, 2021, Chloe was announced as a member of Cignature.
  • The girl group Cignature released its 2nd EP Dear Diary Moment on November 30, 2021, and it peaked at #22 on the Gaon Album Chart.
  • On January 17, 2023, Cignature released its 3rd extended play titled My Little Aurora and it peaked at #18 on the Gaon Album Chart. It consists of songs – I’m Okay, Parade, Palace, and Aurora.
  • Chloe’s specialties are playing the flute & piccolo, painting, and focusing often on what is on her mind.
  • She finds it fascinating that fans like celebrities even though they have only seen the celebrities on a screen. She believes that meeting her fans is “really cool”.
  • Some of her hobbies include skateboarding, watching TED Talks, finding restaurants to go to with friends, and making playlists.
  • Chloe hates beans.
  • She has credited Red Velvet‘s Wendy as her role model because she is good at things that are similar to her and Chloe has met her a few times on the radio.
  • According to her Cignature groupmate Belle, Chloe is the most likely member of the girl group to survive a zombie apocalypse as she would be sleeping at home.
  • She is scared of dark places and birds.
  • Chloe refers to herself as a ‘human haribo’.
  • She has been a recipient of President Obama’s President Education Awards Program certificate and the Congressional Award.
  • Her motto is “Dream big, work hard, make it happen.”
